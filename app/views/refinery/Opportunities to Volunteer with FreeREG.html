The aim of FreeREG is to provide a free-to-use, online, searchable database of parish and nonconformist registers of baptisms, marriages and burials for the whole country. Without the invaluable support of our volunteers, this project would not exist.

Transcribers

We will send you images to transcribe, links to specialist software needed (or you can use a pre-formatted spreadsheet) and guidance on how to transcribe. We will provide feedback on your first attempts so that you can see if any changes are needed in order to meet our high quality standards. You can join a friendly support group where other volunteers can help you learn, and in turn you will be able to advise others.

Web Developers and Designers

We are looking for web developers and designers, and people with experience in: accessibility, UX/UI, front-end development and/or back-end development.

FreeREG uses MongoDB, Ruby on Rails, PHP and RefineryCMS. If you have experience with any of these, or would like to learn, please volunteer.
